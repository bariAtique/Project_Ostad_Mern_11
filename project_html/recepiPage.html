Treditional Bangaldeshi Recepi

A classical Northern Bangladeshi Beef Curry Recepi, known as "Beef Bhuna", Usually spicy and saltry, famous for its color and aroma among all age group.

Ingredients

  • 1 KG Beef (Red)
  • 1 KG Onion
  • 100 Gram Ginger
  • 100 gram Garlic
  • 1/4 Green Chili

    • Instractions

    1. Chop the Onion fine
    2. Paste the Gralic and Ginger
    3. Mix well with beef and all the spice
    4. Cook with sufficient oil for 2 hours
